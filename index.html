About Our Company

Founded in 2003, CleanPro has been providing top-tier janitorial services to clients across various industries. Our commitment to excellence, reliability, and attention to detail has made us a trusted name in the cleaning industry.

We believe that a clean environment is essential for productivity, health, and well-being. Using industry-best practices and eco-friendly products, we ensure every space is not just clean, but truly pristine.

  • Fully insured and bonded for your protection
  • Comprehensive background checks on all staff
  • Customized cleaning plans for each client
  • Environmentally friendly cleaning products
  • 24/7 emergency response service
